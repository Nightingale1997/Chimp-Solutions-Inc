PROJECT

What

The [CHIMPANZEE MEMORY TEST] is an interactive online game which quantifies the user‚Äôs cognitive ability, specifically their short-term memory. The users‚Äô results are being compared with the results of other people who played the game, and to the chimpanzee Auymus' results who have an extraordinary short term memory.

Learning outcomes

The purpose of the game is to highlight the cognitive difference between species by giving an example of a task that a chimpanzee is better at than a human.

How

The game is developed using Javascript, jQuery, HTML, CSS, SQL, and PHP.

For who

The Chimpanzee memory test is an Interaction Design Project and was created to be a part of Universeum‚Äôs digital exhibition Quantified Self. The target audience are 12-16 year olds but everyone is of course welcome!
